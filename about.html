About

Here at Buds and Branches we love all things well designed. Our main branch specializes in print media, specifically trypography based projects. However we have budding projects that pop up occasionally for a limited time. These may be the special offerings of guest designers. Handmade objects limited by supply or items you can personalize or you try being a designer. Work with us to create the perfect piece, cards, photoset to take home and you get to put your own stamp on it too!

Designers

Our primary designer was the sites creator Sarah Knowles. Delighting in typography she loves to blend her passion for words and images into unique print items.

We love partnering with other designers to expand our line of products and the feel of what we offer. These designers come and go so keep you're eyes peeled for when someone new is no site.

Products

All of our products were lovingly handcrafted and designed to bring you joy. We love all things that make the world lovelier and want to share that passion with you through our work.

Our products are all printed on the finest quality papers and fabrics. Carefully chosen to protect the environment and give you the most luxurious product possible.
